Our Story

A New Era of Excellence

Great Optimist Nursery/Primary School was established in October 2023 with a burning passion to redefine foundational education in Ota, Ogun State. Located at 7Famous Estate, Ikosi Road, Iyesi, we believe that “Knowledge Shared is Power.” Our approach blends academic rigour with strong moral values, creating an environment where every child discovers their unique potential.

Despite being a young school, we have quickly gained a reputation for individual attention, character formation, and academic progress. Our dedicated team works relentlessly to produce confident leaders who will shape tomorrow's Nigeria.
